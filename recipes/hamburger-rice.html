Cheesy Hamburger Rice Casserole

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until evenly browned.
  3. Stir in the onion and green bell pepper, and cook until tender.
  4. Remove from heat, and mix in the rice, tomatoes, tomato soup, and water.
  5. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Cover, and bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, until rice is tender.
  7. Top with Cheddar cheese, and continue baking 10 minutes, until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Enjoy!
