Quesadilla

Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of chopped red bell pepper

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place one tortilla on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle cheese, onion, green bell pepper, and red bell pepper over the tortilla.
  4. Place the second tortilla on top.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from oven and cut into wedges.

Enjoy!
