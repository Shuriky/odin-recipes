Chicken Parmasan

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a bowl, combine bread crumbs and parmesan cheese.
  3. Dip chicken breasts in crumb mixture to coat.
  4. In a skillet, brown the coated chicken in oil over medium high heat.
  5. Place browned chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Spread spaghetti sauce over chicken and top with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Sprinkle basil over mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.

Enjoy!
